## Focus Products

Good products to find:
  Premium pens and pen sets, fountain pens, mechanical pencils,
  notebooks and journals, planners and diaries, bullet journals,
  highlighter sets, marker sets, coloured pencil sets,
  desk organisers and accessories, washi tape sets,
  sticky notes (premium branded), revision cards, index cards,
  calligraphy sets, watercolour sets and art supplies,
  sketchbooks, stationery gift sets

Avoid even if they pass filters:
  Paper reams, printer consumables, large format paper,
  office furniture, heavy filing equipment,
  aerosol art supplies (spray fixatives, spray paint)

## Notes

  BSR range is tighter (5k-50k) than other niches because:
  1. Amazon Basics dominates the top of Stationery -- 5k floor avoids them
  2. Good branded stationery moves fast -- 50k ceiling is sufficient

  Hazmat check: some art supplies contain solvents.
  SellerAmp confirmation in Skill 2 handles this.
  Watch for: spray fixatives, solvent-based markers, aerosol adhesives.
