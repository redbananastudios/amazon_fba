## Focus Products

Good products to find:
  Dog leads, harnesses, collars, dog toys, cat toys,
  pet grooming brushes and combs, pet shampoo (non-aerosol),
  pet supplements and vitamins, cat litter accessories,
  pet feeding bowls and mats, pet carriers (lightweight),
  aquarium accessories, bird cage accessories,
  small animal bedding, pet dental care products,
  training aids, pet beds (standard size),
  puzzle feeders and enrichment toys,
  flea and tick treatments (non-aerosol -- spot-on or tablets)

Avoid even if they pass filters:
  Live animals, prescription medications, aerosol flea sprays,
  very large heavy items (large crates, large aquarium tanks),
  raw pet food (requires special storage)

## Notes

  Pet Care is a strong niche for FBA -- high repeat purchase,
  brand loyal buyers, and many small UK brands with weak
  Amazon presence making BRAND APPROACH opportunities common.

  Watch for:
  - Flea/tick treatments -- check if aerosol or spot-on/tablet
    (spot-on and tablets are fine, aerosols are excluded)
  - Pet food -- excluded due to storage and weight requirements
  - Prescription items -- will be gated, flag accordingly

  BSR note: 10k-60k is solid for Pet Supplies. The category
  avg is £26 which sits right in our price range.
