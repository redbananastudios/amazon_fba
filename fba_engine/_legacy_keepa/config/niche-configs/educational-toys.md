## Focus Products

Good products to find:
  STEM kits, coding toys, science experiment sets, maths games,
  reading aids, flash cards, phonics games, geography games,
  language learning tools, puzzles with educational angle,
  art and craft with structured learning, microscopes and science tools,
  electronics starter kits for kids, robotics kits

Avoid even if they pass filters:
  General toys with no learning angle (handled by Kids Toys team)
  Heavy items, battery-heavy items
