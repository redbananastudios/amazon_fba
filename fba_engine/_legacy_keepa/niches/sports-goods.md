NICHE: Sports Goods (Tennis and Badminton ONLY)
SHEET TAB: Sports Goods
PRICE RANGE: GBP20-GBP70

SCOPE: Tennis and Badminton products ONLY.
Reject all other sports categories immediately on sight.
Do not include golf, football, gym equipment, cycling,
swimming, or any other sport.

APIFY KEYWORDS:
tennis racket UK, badminton racket, tennis balls,
shuttlecock badminton, tennis grip tape, overgrip tennis,
badminton set garden, tennis bag, racket strings,
badminton net, tennis training aid, ball machine tennis,
badminton shoes (CHECK WEIGHT -- likely exclude),
tennis elbow brace, badminton grip, racket dampener,
tennis court equipment

WEIGHT CHECK:
Check every product carefully for weight.
Rackets, bags, and nets can be borderline heavy.
If a product triggers oversized or heavy FBA fees: EXCLUDE.
Shuttlecocks, grips, strings, balls, and accessories
are almost always fine.

SEASONAL RISK -- HIGH:
This niche has strong summer seasonality.
ALWAYS check 12-month BSR history on Keepa, not just 90 days.
A product with great BSR in March-August may tank in winter.
Flag Seasonal Risk (column T) as Y for any product showing
clear summer peaks in the Keepa chart.
Only mark as N if demand is consistent across all 12 months.
