educational_overlap:
  (exclude handled by subcategory filter above)

## Focus Products

Good products to find:
  Action figures, playsets, building blocks, board games,
  card games, outdoor games, arts and craft sets, puzzles,
  remote control toys, musical toys, bath toys, role play sets,
  construction toys, vehicles, dolls and accessories

Avoid even if they pass filters:
  Heavy ride-on toys, large play structures, anything requiring
  assembly tools or professional installation

  Playmobil, VTech, LeapFrog, Thames and Kosmos, Halilit,

## Notes

  BSR ceiling is wider (80k) because Toys is a broad category.
  Battery exclusion removes most hazmat risk.
  Seasonal check is important -- check 12 month BSR in Skill 3
  for anything that looks suspiciously strong in a short window.
