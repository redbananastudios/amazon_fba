## Focus Products

Good products to find:
  Curl creams, leave-in conditioners, edge control, hair butters,
  hair oils (small bottles only), shampoos, conditioners, hair masks,
  pump-spray detanglers (pump not aerosol), styling gels, hair mousse
  (non-aerosol only), scalp treatments, deep conditioners

Avoid even if they pass filters:
  Relaxer kits, perm kits, bleach kits, hair colour chemicals,
  large volume liquids (500ml+) in fragile glass packaging,
  electrical styling tools

## Notes

  Hazmat risk is high in this category -- the Keepa HazMat=No filter
  handles the obvious cases. SellerAmp confirmation in Skill 2 is the
  final check. Any product with aerosol, spray, or chemical processing
  ingredients should be flagged regardless of filter outcome.
